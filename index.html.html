About Me
The Person Behind the Work

Hi, I'm Nikita — a creative strategist working at the intersection of data, design, and human behavior.

What Drives Me

A constant curiosity about why people do what they do. I'm drawn to the patterns behind decisions, the emotions behind behavior, and the challenge of turning all of that into something meaningful.

How I Think

I don't believe creativity and analytics exist in separate worlds. The work I care about lives in the tension between the two — where logic meets instinct, and insight turns into something tangible.

Where I Thrive

In environments where I can connect the dots across data, strategy, and execution — and where curiosity, collaboration, and building something real are valued over just checking boxes.
