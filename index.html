About Me
The Person Behind the Work

Hi, I'm Nikita — a strategist who works where data, creative, and human behavior actually meet. Not as separate disciplines, but as the same question asked three different ways.

What Drives Me

I'm more interested in why people decide than what they decide. The patterns behind the choice, the emotion underneath the behavior — that's where the real strategy lives. I'd rather question a brief than execute the wrong one cleanly.

How I Think

Creativity and analytics aren't opposites to me — they're a feedback loop. I use data to pressure-test instinct, and instinct to ask better questions of the data. The work I care about changes a decision, not just a deck.

Where I Thrive

In rooms where the question "why are we assuming that?" is welcome — and where curiosity, collaboration, and building something real matter more than looking busy.

A Little More About Me

I'm someone who's always curious, always exploring, always looking for the next thing that makes me feel alive. Whether that's trying a new restaurant, binge-watching anime, getting lost in a good book, or dancing until my feet hurt — I believe the best ideas come from living fully.

I've trained in Kathak for over 11 years and perform Raas competitively, but creativity shows up in everything I do — from the poetry I write to the illustrations I draw to the concerts I lose myself in. I naturally step into leadership when working with others because I genuinely care about making people feel included and supported.

At my core, I'm someone who loves connecting with people, trying new things, and finding inspiration in the everyday. I think that energy is what makes my work feel human — because it comes from someone who actually is.
